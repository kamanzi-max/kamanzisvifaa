About Us

Kamanzi's Vifaa is Tanzania's trusted source for electronic components. We supply Arduino boards, sensors, modules, resistors, capacitors and more to students, engineers, and hobbyists across the country.

Founded in Dar es Salaam, our mission is simple — make quality electronics accessible and affordable for every Tanzanian who wants to build, create, and innovate.

Whether you are a university student working on a final project, a professional engineer, or someone who just loves electronics — we have what you need, at prices that make sense.
